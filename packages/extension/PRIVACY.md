# HireAll Extension Privacy Policy

*Last updated: December 25, 2024*

## Introduction

HireAll ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Account Information**: When you sign in via Google OAuth, we receive your email address and profile name for authentication purposes.
- **Job Data**: Job listings you choose to save, including job title, company name, location, job URL, and any notes you add.

### Data Collected Automatically
- **LinkedIn Job Listings**: When you're on LinkedIn job pages, the extension reads job post content (title, company, description) only when you click to save.
- **Usage Data**: Basic analytics about extension usage (e.g., feature usage counts) to improve the product.

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect your browsing history
- We do NOT read your private messages
- We do NOT collect data from pages you don't explicitly interact with
- We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising

## How We Use Your Information

- **Sync Jobs**: To sync your saved jobs across devices via your HireAll account
- **Sponsorship Detection**: To check if companies are listed as UK visa sponsors
- **Analytics**: To improve extension features and fix bugs
- **Account Management**: To authenticate and manage your account

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Job data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- **Cloud Storage**: If you sign in, data syncs to Firebase (Google Cloud) servers located in the US/EU
- **Encryption**: All data is transmitted via HTTPS encryption

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share data with third-party advertisers
- Use your data for targeted advertising

We may share data only with:
- **Google Firebase**: For authentication and data storage
- **Law Enforcement**: If required by law

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data via Settings
- **Export**: Export your job data in CSV/JSON format

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `activeTab` | Read job details from the current LinkedIn page when you click Save |
| `storage` | Store your job data and preferences locally |
| `tabs` | Detect when you're on a job page to show relevant UI |
| `identity` | Enable Google Sign-In for account sync |
